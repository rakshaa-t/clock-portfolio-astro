  Working well:
  - The masonry library with real covers feels authentic — varying heights give

  - The e-ink flash on transitions is a great skeuomorphic detail

  - No visual distinction in the library between books you have notes for vs

  Bigger picture: The kindle section is the most interactive of your four
  sections, which is great — it gives people something to explore. The depth
  (library → detail → highlights) rewards curiosity without overwhelming on
  first glance.

  Want me to address any of these, or are you happy with it as-is for now?

  addition since right now you have to click a cover just to know which book it
  is.
